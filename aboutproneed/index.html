Nutritional Health

PRONEED is An organisation that provide Nutrition and education help for children In Haiti. We provide supplement help for undernourished Children according to World Health Organization growth charts, they are enrolled in our program. Our health lessons promote exclusive breastfeeding until 6 months, and all pregnant and lactating mothers are enrolled in the program and received nutritional supplements. In addition to teaching lessons on nutrition and health, local coordinators educate families on child cognitive development. In areas where needed, we also provide semi-annual de-worming of children.
